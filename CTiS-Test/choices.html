Choices Related to Autonomous Robotics In Covid-19:

Reduce of Costs, Robots tend to be cheaper than humans over time. So if a company needs to cut costs or to achieve a certain level of returns for their shareholders, they may choose to use robots rather than humans. For example a $200,000 robot could take the place of 10 people working who have salaries of $100,000 each. This would save $800,000. The robot would also increase productivity and remove human error. High Quality or Low Quality, Many of the top robot manufacturers tend to be more on the more pricey side therefore whoever is going to implement autonomous robots needs to decide whether they want to spend a lot of money to get the best there is or to save costs and get a cheaper option. However, using cheaper robots may reduce the performance and may not be that good of an option over time.
